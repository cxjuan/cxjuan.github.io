About

I am a doctoral candidate at Kyushu University specializing in cybersecurity and artificial intelligence. My research focuses on detecting and defending against Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs), especially in post-compromise data exfiltration scenarios. I also work on reinforcement learning for penetration testing and secure AI applications.
